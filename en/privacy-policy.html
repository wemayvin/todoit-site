Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 24, 2026

wemayvin ("the Developer") values your privacy and is committed to handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how the toDoit app ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information, in accordance with applicable privacy laws including the Korean Personal Information Protection Act.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information. All data (tasks, pages, etc.) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

2. Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect the following information to improve the service and diagnose errors:

  • Firebase Analytics: App usage statistics (screen views, events, and other non-identifiable information)
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash logs (device model, OS version, error details)

This information is processed through Google Firebase and is collected in a form that does not identify individual users.

3. Advertising

The App may include advertisements delivered through Google AdMob in the future.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since the App does not collect personal information, no separate retention or deletion procedures apply. Data stored on your device is deleted when you uninstall the App.

5. Your Rights

You may delete all data stored on your device at any time by uninstalling the App.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, we will notify you through an App update or by updating this page.
